## Findings

For pure sticker price, Host4Fun, Cloudzy, Contabo, Vultr, and Linode look like the most aggressive low-end options among the sources gathered. [host4fun](https://www.host4fun.com/singapore-city-vps)
For value per dollar, Contabo and OVHcloud stand out because their entry tiers expose much larger RAM and storage allocations than the usual 1 GB developer VM offerings. [ovhcloud](https://www.ovhcloud.com/en-sg/vps/vps-singapore/)
For developer workflow and Docker friendliness, OVHcloud explicitly markets Docker VPS, while DigitalOcean, Vultr, Linode, and SimplerCloud all present VM products that are straightforward to use for self-managed Docker or Compose deployments. [cantech](https://www.cantech.in/blog/top-vps-hosting-providers-in-singapore-2026/)

## Pros and cons

### 1) Host4Fun
- Pros: Lowest advertised Singapore entry price found; KVM virtualization; root access; Linux and Windows options. [host4fun](https://www.host4fun.com/singapore-city-vps)
- Cons: I could not verify a formal uptime SLA or detailed base-plan capacity from the gathered sources; some ultra-low pricing may depend on annual billing on related Host4Fun pages. [host4fun](https://www.host4fun.com/asia-vps)

### 2) Cloudzy
- Pros: Very low entry price; explicit Singapore VPS page; entry plan details are clear; NVMe/SSD and flexible upgrades are emphasized. [cloudzy](https://cloudzy.com/singapore-vps/)
- Cons: Some performance and uptime claims came from secondary/review-style sources rather than the provider page; pricing presentation across pages is a bit inconsistent. [cloudzy](https://cloudzy.com/pricing/)

### 3) Contabo
- Pros: Outstanding raw resources for price; Singapore location page exists; unlimited traffic appears on pricing. [contabo](https://contabo.com/en/locations/asia-singapore/)
- Cons: Storage media/options vary by plan; benchmark commentary suggests some Singapore offers may have lower bandwidth/value trade-offs than headline specs imply. [contabo](https://contabo.com/en/pricing/)

### 4) Vultr
- Pros: Strong low-end pricing; hourly billing; several compute families; solid for Docker, CI, and general app hosting. [simplercloud](https://www.simplercloud.com/singapore-vps/)
- Cons: Singapore-specific plan pricing was not shown directly on Vultr’s pricing page in the gathered material; cheapest regular plan uses older “regular performance” hardware. [vpsbenchmarks](https://www.vpsbenchmarks.com/compare/vultr)

### 5) Linode
- Pros: Clear low-end developer-friendly plan; hourly billing; known for straightforward VM usage and predictable overage pricing. [cheapvpslist](https://cheapvpslist.com/v/linode-1024-singapore-sg-123)
- Cons: The fetched pricing page did not expose the small-instance table directly, so the specific $5 Singapore starting point came from a secondary pricing/location source. [vncoupon](https://vncoupon.com/top-low-end-singapore-vps-providers/)

### 6) SimplerCloud
- Pros: Explicitly Singapore-hosted; HA failover and 24/7 monitoring are useful differentiators for local workloads; easy root-level self-management. [simplercloud](https://www.simplercloud.com/prices/)
- Cons: Base capacity details were not fully exposed in the gathered snippets; add-ons like extra transfer and extra disk can raise effective cost. [simplercloud](https://www.simplercloud.com/singapore-vps/)

### 7) OVHcloud
- Pros: Very strong specs for the money in Singapore; daily backups included; unlimited traffic; explicit Docker VPS support. [ovhcloud](https://www.ovhcloud.com/en-sg/vps/)
- Cons: Pricing is in SGD ex-GST, so direct comparison with USD/EUR competitors needs normalization; some larger plans were marked sold out. [ovhcloud](https://www.ovhcloud.com/en-sg/vps/)

### 8) UpCloud
- Pros: Good reputation for transparent pricing and performance orientation; fixed pricing and no-cost data transfer messaging is attractive. [upcloud](https://upcloud.com/pricing/)
- Cons: Entry price is higher than the cheapest budget hosts; I could not directly verify a Singapore-specific low-end plan within the gathered pricing excerpts. [upcloud](https://upcloud.com/vps-hosting/)

### 9) DigitalOcean
- Pros: Mature platform, predictable billing, good Docker/Kubernetes ecosystem, and Singapore data-centre presence cited by recent comparison material. [digitalocean](https://www.digitalocean.com/solutions/vps-hosting)
- Cons: Not among the cheapest for comparable RAM once you move beyond the absolute entry tier; provider page snippet emphasizes premium $7 plan more than the $4 global floor. [digitalocean](https://www.digitalocean.com/solutions/vps-hosting)

### 10) Hetzner
- Pros: Very attractive global pricing baseline and strong included networking/security features. [costgoat](https://costgoat.com/pricing/hetzner)
- Cons: Singapore is explicitly more expensive than Hetzner’s EU and US regions, so it stops being a price leader once you insist on Singapore placement. [costgoat](https://costgoat.com/pricing/hetzner)

## Recommendation by use case

If your top priority is the absolute cheapest Singapore footprint for experiments, tiny apps, or a personal Docker host, start with Host4Fun or Cloudzy, then sanity-check renewal terms and support responsiveness before committing. [getapp](https://www.getapp.sg/software/2073591/cloudzy)
If you want the best low-cost resource bundle, Contabo and OVHcloud are stronger value picks than most 1 GB starter VMs. [ovhcloud](https://www.ovhcloud.com/en-sg/vps/vps-singapore/)
If you care more about developer experience, ecosystem, and predictable operations than squeezing the last dollar, Vultr, Linode, DigitalOcean, and OVHcloud are the safer shortlists. [ovhcloud](https://www.ovhcloud.com/en-sg/vps/os/vps-docker/)

## Practical caution

For a Docker workload in Singapore, the lowest price is not always the best total cost, because limited bandwidth, weaker support, missing backups, or slower storage can erase the savings once the service goes into production. [readyserver](https://www.readyserver.sg/blog/cheap-vps-singapore-best-value-hosting/)
Also note that some sources here are direct provider pages while others are third-party comparison or benchmark sites, so you should re-check final cart pricing, tax, billing cycle, and region-specific availability before purchase. [vpsbenchmarks](https://www.vpsbenchmarks.com/compare/contabo)
